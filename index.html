How It Works

Four steps to become a Hunter

No gear, no grind — just your phone and the world around you.

1

Explore the map

A live, GPS-powered map turns your real surroundings into a world of realms, quests, and hidden treasure.

2

Scan with the Trash-Cam

Spot litter? Point your AI Trash-Cam at it. It identifies the trash and marks it as a target.

3

Clean it up

Pick it up, bin it, snap the proof. Real-world action is the only way to power up in the game.

4

Heal & collect

Watch realms come back to life, earn rewards, and hatch, raise, and evolve your own Elemonsters.
